    • How to play the Paper-Scissor-Rock game

    Play Paper-Scissor-Rock game against computer

    (CityU Scope ITB, March 2020)

    You will be playing Paper-Scissor-Rock against the computer. Player needs to enter name to play. Once name is entered, Paper-Scissor-Rock images will apear and player will pick his/her choice by clicking the one of the images


    The main screen has Paper-Scissor-Rock images changing in high speed. When the player pick the image from the right, the computer will randaomly select one to compare agianst.


    The main screen will show computer choice and your choice. If you win, the screen diaplays "You win!". If you lose, It will display "You lose!", otherwise, it will diaplay "You are as smart as the computer!"


    At the bottom part of the main section displays the score. The winner earns a point but no point is lost for tie or loser. If the player wants to play agian, he/she can click the "Play Again " button or just simply click the choice on one of the paper-scissor-rock images


    Have fun and enjoy!
